Featured County Art Artist

'Manchester Rising'

Fascinated by the ever changing landscape in and around Manchester.

Capturing the soul of the industrial North

Days of yesteryear

Remember them? David creates captivating paintings that depict nostalgic scenes and individuals spanning across Manchester and Salford. His works are characterized by a blend of simplicity and dynamism, each carrying a unique narrative. With a distinct style, David has garnered a dedicated following of collectors both within the UK and internationally.

Northern memories

Paintings that take a snapshot of shared moments and memories preserving them, before time whisks them away. Bringing to life the atmosphere of cherished locations and individuals in and around Manchester and Salford. Infused with simplicity and vitality, each piece carries its own captivating narrative.

Industrial power

A view into the past, when the North really was an industrial powerhouse.

Wonderfully atmospheric paintings

David's vibrant, multi-layered acrylics on board, perfectly capture moments of a northen bygone era.
